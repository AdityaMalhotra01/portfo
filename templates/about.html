About me

For the past 3 years, I've been studying data science using mostly python. I love what I learn as every day there is something new and exciting opportunities to learn. I possess the knowledge of Python, VBA and SQL.

In my spare time, the artificial intelligence and machine learning development community is a big part of my life. Whether doing projects or blogs, it helps me stay up to date.

Besides programming I love spending time with my friends and family and can often be found together going out catching the latest movie, staying in playing games on the sofa or planning a trip to someplace I've never been before.

I spend a lot of time learning with communities and groups about new techniques advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning and advanced algorithms.
